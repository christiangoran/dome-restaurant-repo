About Us

Dome Lombok is a small business located in the beautiful Gerupuk Bay, Lombok. We offer a variety of accommodation options to suit all budgets, from our luxury dome to our smaller friendly domes. We also offer a range of activities including surf lessons, yoga classes, fishing trips, snorkelling trips and more. We are passionate about providing our guests with a unique and memorable experience and we look forward to welcoming you to our little slice of paradise.
